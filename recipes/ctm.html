Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken tikka masala is a dish that comes from India and Pakistan. It is made from a mix of vegetables, spices, and chunks of chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • Masala spices
  • Chicken chunks
  • Vegetable puree

Steps

  1. Spice chicken and cook it
  2. Spice vegetable puree
  3. Mix together
